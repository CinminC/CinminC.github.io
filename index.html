Who is, the artist?

This generative art work explores the differences between the way that computer and human form the composition. The artwork offers two distinct experiences. The first involves human intervention. The second approach involves only programming and algorithmic execution, which automatically completing the entire image. By employing these two different painting methods, the contrasting thought processes between humans and computers can be observed.

SOUNDMATE

SOUNDMATE is specially designed for couples or families who are not able to see each other every day. The sound from their living spaces would be converted to real-time audio visualization displaying on the screen through programming, which provides a remote and privacy-keeping way to maintain the relationship between you and your significant other.
